Mantaining a work-life balance is hard but necessary one. Some things I do in my off time are:

Soccer

Soccer improves our physical health. It's a game that demands agility, endurance, and strength. From running across the field to executing precise kicks, every aspect of soccer contributes to our overall fitness. But soccer isn't just about physical fitness; it's also about mental and emotional well-being. Soccer teaches us the importance of teamwork, discipline, and resilience. On the field, we learn to communicate effectively, collaborate with others, and support our teammates.

Gym

Gym sessions provide an excellent opportunity to de-stress and unwind from the pressures of daily life. Exercise releases endorphins, often referred to as "feel-good" hormones, which can alleviate stress, anxiety, and even symptoms of depression. Moreover, the gym fosters discipline and consistency. Committing to a regular workout schedule requires dedication and perseverance.

Gaming

Even tho gaming is still frowing apon in most people eyes they offer numerous benefits that can significantly enrich our lives. Gaming provides and avenue for relaxations and stress relief. Also gaming fosters creativity and problem-solving skills. Many games require players to think critically, strategize, and make quick decisions to overcome challenges and achieve objectives
